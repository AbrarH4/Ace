⚡ PHYSICS: LAWS OF MOTION
1. Newton’s First Law

A body remains at rest or in uniform motion unless acted upon by external force.

👉 Also called law of inertia

2. Newton’s Second Law

Force = mass × acceleration
F = ma

More mass → more force needed
More force → more acceleration
3. Newton’s Third Law

For every action, there is an equal and opposite reaction.

Example:

Walking
Rocket launch
4. Momentum

Momentum = mass × velocity

Symbol: p
Unit: kg m/s
5. Law of Conservation of Momentum

Total momentum before = total momentum after (if no external force)

6. Types of Inertia
Inertia of rest
Inertia of motion
Inertia of direction

Example:
Sudden braking → passengers fall forward

7. Friction
Opposes motion
Depends on surface roughness
Can be useful (walking) or harmful (wear & tear)
