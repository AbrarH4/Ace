⚗️ CHEMISTRY: ATOMIC STRUCTURE
1. Basic Idea

Everything is made of atoms. Atom is the smallest unit of an element.

2. Subatomic Particles
Particle	Charge	Location
Proton	+1	nucleus
Neutron	0	nucleus
Electron	-1	shells
3. Atomic Number & Mass Number
Atomic number (Z) = number of protons
Mass number (A) = protons + neutrons
4. Electron Arrangement

Shells follow:

K shell = 2
L shell = 8
M shell = 18 (generally)

Example: Sodium (11)
→ 2, 8, 1

5. Isotopes

Same element, different neutrons.

Example:

Carbon-12
Carbon-14
6. Bohr Model (Key Idea)
Electrons move in fixed orbits
Energy is quantized
Jump between levels = energy absorbed/released
7. Valency
Combining capacity of an atom
Example:
Oxygen = 2
Hydrogen = 1
